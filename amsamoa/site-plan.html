Content

Home page

Welcome to Samoa! Samoa has a rich and fascinating history and culture to be explored, spanning more than 3,000 years. The traditional culture is not for tourist attractions but a way of life that the people of Samoa continue to live and nuture. Most aspects of the the Samoan culture from a millenium ago is still reflected in Samoan culture and society today. The fa'asamoa emphasizes community and looking after one another. Because of this communal way of living, the people are very sociable, and welcoming with their trademark smiles. They are known throught Polynesia as the "happy people" for their enjoyment of life and their good spirited nature! Through their traditional dancing, delicious cooking, music, well perserved arts and craft, Samoa offers visitors a wide range of authentric experiences to discover and immerse themselves in the Samoan culture and way of life. Come experience our warm and rich culture and discover breathtaking scenery, heavenly beaches and mesmerizing waterfalls. Samoa is renowned for its natural beauty and genuine hospitality, the perfect island destination for your next trip with friends and families to create special memories to last a lifetime.

Experience

A culture that is traditionally rich based on fa'asamoa, or the Samoan way of life, there are plenty of opportunities for visitors to enjoy a touching experience. In Samoa, getting around the island is part of the holiday experience! From colorful local buses, or road tripping in the comfort of your own rental car, to cruising ferry's this is an experience of a lifetime. experience page, visitors can learn about different types of transport and how to get around the island. From terminals located at Mulifanua Wharf you can take the ferry to get to the two main islands, Upolu and Savaii. As you get around the island, your encounters with nature will be apart of everything you do in Samoa. Depending on your level of expertise and area of interest, there are many jungle trails and hikes, waterholes , marine life and many more adventures that await you. To be prepared, it is reccomended to pack light & breathable fabrics as the temperature throughout the year rangers from 24 to 30 degrees Celsius. The water temperature is also perfect all year round for snorkeling and swimming.

Accomodations

Samoa has all the makings of the perfect romantic escape or family friendly vacation. Samoa offers an oasis of uninteruppted peace and picture-perfect beaches with gentle waves and coconut palms in this idyllic paradise. The friendly and welcoming people of Samoa adds to this retreat of a lifetime, promising an extra special experience for all visitors and families. The perfect way to experience fa'a Samoa - the Samoan way of life - is the fale accomodations. You'll find anything from private villas, fine dinning restaraunts or luxurious resorts along the coastline of Samoa. Traditional cuisine and good food is an enormous part of local culture in Samoa. Not only are the celebrations big but so are the feasts and people full of life. The essense of Samoan food is made with love and enjoyed together with extended families. This is one of the greatest joys of Samoan life which you can experience as you enjoy your stay at the local accomodations. There's no shortage of beaches in Samoa, and the calm and clear warm waters will give you a rare opportunity to experience island life at its finest. Most fales gets you as close to the ocean as possible for a refreshing breath of crisp ocean breeze and relaxation. Most accomodations come with reasonable prices for breakfast and dinner included.

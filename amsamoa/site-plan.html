Home page

American Samoa is renowned for its natural beauty and genuine hospitality, the perfect island destination for your next trip with friends and families to create special memories to last a lifetime.

Images for the Home page

Experience

In Samoa, getting around the island is part of the holiday experience! From colorful local buses, or road tripping in the comfort of your own rental car, to cruising ferry's this is an experience of a lifetime. experience page, visitors can learn about different types of transport and how to get around the island. From terminals located at Mulifanua Wharf you can take the ferry to get to the two main islands, Upolu and Savaii.

Images for the Page 2

Accomodations

The perfect way to experience fa'a Samoa - the Samoan way of life - is the fale accomodations. Most fales gets you as close to the ocean as possible for a refreshing breath of crisp ocean breeze and relaxation. Most accomodations come with reasonable prices for breakfast and dinner included.
